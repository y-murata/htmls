Company History

January 1984 Established Taiyo Seisakusho for electronic components assembly business in the field of electronics
May 1988 Established Yugen Kaisha Taiyo Seisakusho in Nishi Nagano, Kakunodate-machi, Senboku District (current Senboku-shi), Akita Prefecture
June 1991 Change the organization toTaiyo Seisakusho Co., Ltd.
October 1991 Relocated the head office to Kumoshikari, Kakunodate-machi, Senboku District (current Senboku-shi), Akita Prefecture
February 1996 Received the certification of research and development support project based on "Concerning Measures for the Promotion of the Creative Business Activities of Small and Medium Enterprises" of research and development support projects from the Small and Medium Enterprise Agency
March 1997 Received the accreditation of support project based on "Act on Temporary Measures for Facilitating Specific New Business (New Business Law)" from the Ministry of International Trade and Industry
July 1997 Started selling lead frame inspection equipment (MV7000 series)
January 2001 Changed the name to INSPEC INC.
January 2002 Completed the development of special computer "inspecII" for image processing for tape inspection equipment and tape inspection equipment
March 2002 Started selling BGA inspection equipment (BF2000 series) (equipped with inspecII)
July 2002 Started selling TAB tape inspection equipment (TR2000 series) (equipped with inspecII)
March 2003 Established Tokyo office in Minato-ku, Tokyo
March 2005 Completed the development of special computer "inspecIII" for image processing for high-performance pattern inspection equipment
June 2006 Listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Mothers
October 2006 Completed the expansion work in the head office factory
May 2009 Started selling PCB substrate AOI (pattern inspection equipment) SX5000 series having the highest performance in the world
July 2009 Selected for "2009 straetgic fundamental technology support business" by the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry
November 2009 Selected for "Financial aid to small and medium enterprise product development" by the Ministry of Economy, Trade, and Industry
October 2010 Started selling the strategic product SX3300 series, for the global market of PCB substrate AOI
June 2011 Entered into comprehensive agency agreement with Taiwan TKK (Taiwan kong king Co., Ltd.)
August 2011 Launched in-line high performance inspection equipment
October 2012 Launched compact AVI (final visual inspection equipment) AV500 for flexible board (FPC)
November 2012 Selected for "2012 Global Technology Collaboration Project" for the Ministry of Economy, Trade, and Industry
September 2013 Established a local company in Taiwan (Inspec Taiwan Inc.)
October 2014 Established 100% subsidiary company Terra Inc., and entered the medical related devices business
March 2015 A subsidiary of the "First EIE SA" in Geneva, Switzerland
