Products

Automatic visual inspection equipment, automatic measurement equipment, other equipment

After releasing a lead frame inspection equipment in 1996, INSPEC INC. has develop several visual inspection equipment from precision board AOI to semiconductor package board AVI, and supplied them to several users.
We supply excellent AOI and AVI inspection equipment backed by our high level of technologies and abundant know-how from ultra-precision semiconductor package board to HDI board, and we offer the best solution to the ever changing requirements of our customers.

  • AUTOMATIC OPTICAL INSPECTION

    We conduct pattern inspection of various printed boards.
    There are following types based on the configuration of products.

    ◆ Applicable Products

    • ・Semiconductor package board
    • ・Precision printed board
    • ・Glass mask substrate
    • ・Film substrate
    • ・Flexible board
    • ・TAB tape

  • AUTOMATIC FINAL VISUAL INSPECTION

    We conduct final visual inspection of various printed boards.
    Following are the types of applicable products.

    ◆ Applicable products

    • ・Semiconductor package board
    • ・Printed board
    • ・LED board
    • ・Lead frame
    • ・Flexible board (FPC)
    • ・TAB tape

Introduction to inspec's outstanding inspection technologies.

Backed by its outstanding image inspection technologies, inspec responds to advanced inspection requirements with the best cost-performance.

  • Image processing technology

    We can handle this kind of advanced requirements.
