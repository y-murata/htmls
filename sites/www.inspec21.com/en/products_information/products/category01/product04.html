Glass mask AOI　SX6000G

Line/space　　４／４、６／６

SX6000G is a glass mask AOI that can support fine pattern with L/W up to 4/4.
With length measurement algorithm, it detects mask specific detects without fail.

Features of SX6000G

  • ・High sensitivity and high-speed processing
  • ・High precision inspection by the sub-pixel measurement processing algorithm by gray image
  • ・It can handle all patterns, and it measures and determines wire width and space

Inspection time (※)

・Please contact us regarding inspection time.

Maximum workpiece size 610mm×610mm
