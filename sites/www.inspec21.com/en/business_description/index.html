Business Description

High end optical visual inspection equipment manufacturer in the IT field

INSPEC INC. is a manufacturer of visual inspection equipment that automatically conducts visual inspection of semiconductor and various IT related devices. Backed by persistent development efforts and years of experience, we have excellent image processing technologies, optical sensing technologies, and mechatronics technologies, which are the core technologies of visual inspection equipment, We develop visual inspection equipment having the highest level of performance covering up to high-end requirements, and we offer the best solution for the advanced inspection needs.
Today, performance of exponentially advancing IT devices is realized by sophisticated built-in electronic devices. As important tools responsible for quality assurance of these electronic devices, INSPEC always offer visual inspection equipment having the highest level of performance.
