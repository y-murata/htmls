We offer promotional marketing for both domestic and international user acquisition. Our services include reward-based device app and website advertising for smartphones and tablets, advertising through ad networks, and app store optimization (ASO) consulting, targeting our advertisers, media owners, and developers. We are constantly developing new technologies and providing new services, keeping pace with the growth of smartphone and tablet markets.

Mobile Marketing Services >>

We offer online to offline (O2O) services that guide acquired users through the Internet to physical retail stores. The majority of these users come from smartphone apps.
Apps can be used to acquire customers and guide them to physical locations through the use of in-app promotional benefits such as stamp cards, coupons, and reservations.
