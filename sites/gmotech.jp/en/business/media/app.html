Part-time Now!

Part-time Now! is one of the biggest all-purpose job offer information portals in the industry. It provides job offer and work information on part-time jobs and seasonal employment. It immediately provides helpful job offer information for those looking for a part-time job. Part-time Now! is based on information provided by From A Navi.

FindWork, a Career Change Search App with Job Offer Information

FindWork is an app that can search job offers and employment opportunity information nationwide. This is perfect if you are job hunting or thinking of a career change! It can filter job offer information by specifying various conditions such as the prefectural area, the railway line/station, the job type, the particular conditions, and the use of free words.

Gourmet Review, Foodie App with Value for Coupons

Gourmet Review is a value for coupon app for foodies. Not only can it help you to find options for today's lunch or dinner, but it can also search for specific things you may be looking for, such as a restaurant with a great night view or a sunken kotatsu.

Car Now! Used Car Information App

A general information app for used cars. Search for or value used cars easily and simply. You can search among 200,000 used cars. Furthermore, detailed search filters can help you find the car you are looking for based on price, mileage, or body color. With a smartphone, you can access the latest used car information anytime, anywhere.
