The President's Message

GMO TECH, a member of the GMO Internet, Inc. Group, is a pioneer in the user acquisition business.

Chief among our offerings to our customers are:

  • · GMO SmaAD Network—An ad network created specifically for smartphone apps
  • · GMO SmaAD Reward—A reward network created specifically for smartphone apps
  • · GMO AppCapsule—An application service provider based content management system to easily generate smartphone apps
  • · SEO AIRLINES by GMO—Search engine optimization
  • · Advertising—Creating and maintaining Facebook, listing, retargeting, and display ads

We are growing our business with a focus on conversion results from inquiries, purchases and installs. Such results are the most critical as key performance indicators for our customers' online strategies. This is true for both the online marketing business and the smartphone app business.

The various offerings our company provides are built on a central business model. We provide our clients with results, which equates to revenue.


For example, the business model we operate under with our GMO SmaAD offerings, a part of our mobile marketing services, dictates that everything we provide contributes to client revenue. This comes in the form of monetized results, either through cost-per-install (CPI) or cost-per-acquisition (CPA) offerings.

We can logically tote up the numbers when it comes to our Internet marketing offerings for PCs and cellphones. Once numerical targets are achieved, we believe that our greatest goal can be met: maintaining mutual profitability for both our customers and ourselves.
Take search engine optimization. Boosting your search ranking is not the be all and end all of the process.
We analyze chosen keywords and the existence of search markets. We can then contribute to revenue tied to actual profits based on simulations that take into account incoming users, and purchase and subscription rates.
We believe that we are offering our customers a meaningful service when indeed those purchase and subscription rates are high.


We are positioned to uniquely be of service to companies that require Internet user acquisition, and, in particular, smartphone user acquisition.
We welcome the opportunity to work with you.


Akito Suzuki, President, GMO TECH
