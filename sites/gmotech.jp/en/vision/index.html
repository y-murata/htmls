Management philosophy

Be a company where all staffs are materially and spiritually rich,
able to create “smile” and “inspiration,”contributing to the growth of the society through skills,
knowledge and activities of the entire staffs, and be a company that always acts ethically

Corporate vision

Be an Internet marketing company that continues to create services, which are essential to people worldwide.

Operational vision

Be the representative smartphone user acquisition company in Japan.

Business strategy

Aspire to be a leader in install type
advertisements on smartphone apps.

We perform promotions and marketing, such as reward advertisements for smartphones (apps, websites)/tablets, ad network advertising, and ASO consulting that attract both domestic and international customers for our advertising clients, media owners, and developers. We are constantly developing new technologies and providing new services, keeping pace with the growth of smartphone/tablet markets.

Aspire to be a leader in
publishing smartphone apps.

We offer O2O services (online to offline services) that guide users attracted through the Internet, mostly from smartphone apps, to physical retail stores.
This app can attract customers and guide them to physical retail stores through functions such as “stamp card,”“coupon,”or“reservation,”which promote store visits.
