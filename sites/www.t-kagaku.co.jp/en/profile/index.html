Message from the President and CEO

Tohoku Chemical Cooperation is a specialized trading company that deals mainly with chemical reagents, clinical diagnostic reagents, and their related devices. We are developing business based in Tohoku regions. We have been establishing approximately 7,000 accounts including manufactures, plants, universities and self-governing hospitals. Nowadays we meet highly sophisticated and diversified needs.

Our business consists largely of the Chemical Reagent & Machinery Group and the Clinical Diagnostic Reagent & Machinery Group.
In the former group, we are increasing significantly the sales of chemical reagents utilized in the process of producing semiconductors and liquid crystals recently. Furthermore, the sales of neutralizers necessary for improving soil and asbestos-related equipment are going up well, indicating that the needs reflect environmental conservations that each company tackles. In the latter group, most of the sales cover the merchandises to meet demands for medical system.

The most important point in our business is that we grasp their needs deeply and that we provide clients with expertise through communication. To achieve this, we devote our continuous effort to live up to client’s expectations.
We are capable of addressing various problems with our clients by providing one-stop service and supplying merchandise extensively.
Finally thanks to all of our clients, TOHOKU Chemical Cooperation marked its 60th anniversary last year.
We would like to thank you for your continued support and patronage.
