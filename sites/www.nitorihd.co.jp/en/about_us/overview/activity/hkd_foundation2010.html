Hokkaido Aid Fund

In appreciation of Hokkaido’s support for Nitori.

Nitori started in 1967 with only 30 tsubo (1 tsubo = 3.3m²) of store area; now we have 226 stores (as of October 31). We would like to thank our customers in Hokkaido for their continued support.
Same as last year, this year we are also offering financial aid to help improve Hokkaido, our home prefecture, with the wish to ‘vitalize Hokkaido that has supported Nitori’.

See Application Guidelines for details about how to apply.
