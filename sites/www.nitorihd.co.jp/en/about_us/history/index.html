Corporate History

Nitori’s history and changes are described below.

1967

December

“NITORI Furniture Store” Established
1972 March

“NITORI Furniture Wholesale Center Co., Ltd” (2 stores) Founded
1975 December

“Nango Store”, Japan’s first air dome construction, opened as a furniture store in Shiroishi Ward Sapporo (moved to the Shiroishi Store in December, 1980)
1978 June

Company name changed to “NITORI Furniture Co., Ltd”
1980 August

 

“Distribution Center” moved to Teine District, Sapporo and automated warehouse opened to save labor and maintain storage Head office attaching to the distribution center moved to its present location
1981 June

“Tomakomai Store” opened in Tomakomai as the first store outside of Sapporo
1986 May

Direct import of overseas products started
  July

Company name changed to “NITORI Co., Ltd” and store name to “Home Furnishing NITORI”
  October

“One house proposal” started based on the idea of O.T.C.M.
1988 April

“Store Development Department Kanto Office” established in Matsudo, Chiba
1989 March

“N.T. Singapore PTE, Ltd.” Established as a Singapore-based affiliate
  September

Listed in Sapporo Stock Exchange
1990 February

New Head Office constructed
  July

“Kyushu Distribution Center” opened in Ohkawa, Fukuoka
1992 October

Awarded by the Minister of International Trade and Industry as an outstanding international trader
1993 March

“Katsuta Store” opened in Hitachinaka, Ibaraki as the first store in Honshu
1995 September

Expanded the Distribution Center and established the most advanced automatic storage system. (Total floor area 10,000m2, 14 of automatic storage system, 4,300 of racks)
  November

“Kanto Distribution Center” opened in Funabashi, Chiba
1998 March

Store name changed to “Home Fashion NITORI”
1999 July

 “Socia Store” the first Home Fashion store opened
  September

“Crystal Mall Nikonikodo NITORI” (Present Yume-town Kumamoto store) opened in the shopping center in Kumamoto as the first store in Kyushu
  November

“Yume Town Takamatsu Store” opened in Takamatsu as the first store in Shikoku
2000 July

 “Kanto Distribution Center” reorganized and moved to Shiraoka, Saitama
  August

Marumitsu Co., Ltd fully subsidized
  November

“Aeon Gushikawa Store” opened as the first store in Okinawa
2001 October

Construction started on the expansion of the Kanto Distribution Center to 112,540m2
2002 April

“Kaizuka Store” opened in Kaizuka as the first store in Osaka
  August

The Second stage construction of Kanto Distribution Center completed and full operation started
  October

Listed in Tokyo Stock Exchange
2003 November

“Construction of Kansai Distribution Center” started in Port Island in Kobe, Hyogo
  December

“Utsunomiya Store” opened, achieving the target of 100 stores
Construction of “Marumitsu Vietnam Factory” started in Hanoi
2004 January

Surpassed sales of 100 billion Yen
  March

“Pinghu Distribution Center” opened in Shanghai, China
The first Japanese company Licensed for and established of 100% joint venture based on the Foreign Business Investment Export Supply Center Management Law
Conducted the 5% reduction in price of all products with total labeling system of consumption tax.
  September

Advanced into the Internet Shopping business
  October

Trial production at the Marumitsu Vietnam Factory started
  November

Started full operation of “Kansai Distribution Center”
Donated 100 million Yen to the victims of Niigate Chuetsu Earthquake
2005 January

Received the “Expansion of individual Shareholder Award” from the Tokyo Stock Exchange
Donated 200 million Yen to the victims of the Sumatra Earthquake through the Japan Red Cross Society
  February

NITORI Hokkaido Aid Fund established
A portion of profits applied for the aid fund in Hokkaido
  July

The scholarship ceremony of “NITORI International Scholarship Foundation” and gathering party held
  November

A certification of the international standard for the quality management system,”ISO9001:2000” received
“NITORI endowed chair course” established to revitalize the local economy of Hokkaido as a social contribution activity.
2006 April

“Akabane Store” opened in Kita-ku, Tokyo.
  June

Construction of “Huizhou Distribution Center” started in Huizhou, China
  July

Tokyo Head Quarters newly located on the 6th and 7th floor of “Akabane Store”
  October

“Overseas trade business policy meeting” held (the first holding in Japan). The first “NITORI World Circle” (NWC) held (small group activities)
  December

Yubari Project of “Shiawase-no-Sakura-to Momiji” executed as the Reconstruction Support of Yubari city
The project of planting 20,000 trees in Yubari launched
2007 January

The first world “NWC” meeting held
  May

The first overseas store “Kaohsiung Dream Time store” opened in Kaohsiung, Taiwan
Akio Nitori (president) entitled the Honorary Consul of France in Sapporo
  October

Started selling “Integrated Kitchen System” business. Entered the market with Home Improvement Work
The second “NWC” held (small group activities)
  December

Became the main sponsor of the Consadole J-League soccer club
2008 May

Started “the 1, 3 and 5-year warranty service” on furniture
  June

The first “Price Reduction Declared” started
  November

Awarded silver prize of “Excellent Product Safety Measure Firm” by the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry
2009 March

Started the outlet shops and NITORI NET
  September

“Lalaport Misato Store” opened, achieving the target of 200 stores
2010 May

Another “Price Reduction Declared” conducted, reduced the prices of 3,400 items in total
  August

Reorganized to a Holding Company. Changed the corporate name and organization NITORI Co., Ltd → NITORI Holdings Co., Ltd
2011 March

Accomplished to open stores in 47 prefectures by “Matsue Store” in Shimane
Donated 1.8 billion Yen for the victims of the Great East Japan earthquake
