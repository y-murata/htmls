New Store Site Proposals

Thanks to the patronage of its customers, Nitori has grown into a national chain developing its business throughout Japan.
To actively expand our store network in every region of Japan in the future, we are looking for owners who have studied effective land use.
If you have a potential store site you would like to propose, please feel free to contact Nitori at any time. We look forward to your cooperation.
