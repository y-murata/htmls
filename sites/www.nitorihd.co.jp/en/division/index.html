The Nitori Group’s Businesses

Nitori group has continued to grow by mapping out a long-term vision that is global in scope, and based on our desire to offer the affluence of western homes to the Japanese people.

Through its stores throughout Japan, Nitori sells furniture and interior goods that enable it to provide “the True Comfortable Living Style” to people wherever they live.  At Nitori, we offer products that can be “totally coordinated” by our customers, at affordable prices, to help each customer realize an affluent, comfortable living style.

Through steps to lower intermediate costs, Home Logistics helps Nitori achieve affordable prices for its products. Its efforts include the construction of an efficient chain of logistics centers to ensure prompt delivery of goods to Nitori’s nationwide network of stores and low delivery costs, and the warehousing of products developed overseas.

http://www.homelogi.co.jp

Nitori Furniture Co., Ltd. manufactures a variety of Nitori furniture such as chests of drawers, storage chests, lowboards, sideboards, kichen cabinets, home electrical appliance stands and beds. The company is also aggressively developing overseas strategies, including materials procurement and technical guidance, to enable Nitori to offer even better products at even lower prices.

As a member of the Nitori Group, NITORI PUBLIC. provides advertising and public relations support and growth strategies for each company, including Nitori. Through advertising communications, this advertising agency is working to develop new communications strategies and create value with the goal of “becoming clients’ most trusted branding partner.”

We are responsible for the maintenance of Nitori Group’s stores and offices, ensuring they are always kept in the best conditions. We also strive to create comfortable store space for our customers, and to reduce costs.
As an insurance agency, we provide non-life and life insurances to our customers and employees, supporting them to help them create a reassuring and satisfactory life plan.
